About Me

Discover the journey that led me from curious Free State dreamer to passionate tech professional

My Tech Journey

My journey into tech wasn't traditional. Growing up in Welkom, Free State, I didn’t have access to coding bootcamps or tech mentors — only an endless curiosity and a determination to figure things out on my own. I was that kid who loved taking apart radios just to see how they worked. That curiosity led me to study Electrical Engineering at the Central University of Technology, where I earned my Diploma in Engineering Technology in Electrical Engineering.

University was where I first discovered programming — a spark that quickly turned into passion. Coming from a community where tech opportunities are limited, I knew I had to create my own path. I taught myself to code using free online resources, balancing late-night learning sessions with my engineering studies. It wasn’t easy, but it taught me resilience, creativity, and independence — qualities that now define my approach to both life and technology.

My professional experience bridges two worlds: the physical systems of engineering and the digital realm of software. During my internship as a Maintenance Technician at Gibela Railway, I worked in high-pressure environments where precision and safety were everything. I developed strong skills in: Fault finding and troubleshooting complex electrical systems High-voltage work and LOTO (Lock Out Tag Out) safety procedures System diagnostics using specialized software and Ethernet communication That hands-on experience shaped my problem-solving mindset. But my heart kept pulling me toward tech innovation. In 2024, I began formally upskilling — earning certificates from CISCO and Deviare, and in 2025, completing the ITVarsity Software Developer program, where I learned to design and build real-world websites and applications.

What Drives Me

Technology, for me, isn't just about code—it's about connection and community. I love how a well-designed website can help a small business owner reach new customers, or how a simple app can solve everyday problems for people in my community. Every project I work on carries a piece of my heart and my commitment to making technology more accessible and meaningful.

I bring a fresh perspective to every challenge because I approach problems differently. Where others see obstacles, I see opportunities to innovate. My background has taught me to be resourceful, to think creatively, and to never give up when something seems impossible.
